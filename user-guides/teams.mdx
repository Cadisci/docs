Lovable lets you collaborate with teammates in real time as you build your app together. Invite your designer, developer, agency, or anyone else to join your workspace.

## Create workspace

Create a new place to make projects or collaborate with others.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Click the **‘Create new workspace’** button in a project or from the dashboard. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    Enter workspace name.

    ![Create Workspace Lovable Pn](/images/create-workspace-lovable.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    Select a plan for your new workspace.

    ![Select Plan Workspace Pn](/images/select-plan-workspace.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Change workspace name

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Insure you are in the right workspace, otherwise, change to the desired Workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    Click on "**Settings**"

    ![Invite Lovable 2 Pn](/images/invite-lovable-2.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    You can now edit the "**Workspace name**" and the "**Workspace description**".

    ![Workspace Setting Pn](/images/workspace-setting.png)

     
  </Step>
</Steps>

## **Invite & Manage Collaborators**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Upgrade plan (if not yet in "Teams")

    ![Upgrade Plan Pn](/images/upgrade-plan.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    Click the **‘Invite’** button in a project or from the dashboard.

    ![Invite Lovable Pn](/invite-lovable-2.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    Enter their email addresses to send invitations.

    ![Invite Collaborator Pn](/images/invite-collaborator.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4">
    When teammates accept their email invitations, they’ll join your workspace and see your projects.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## **Real-time collaboration**

When teammates join your project:

- See their cursor and changes in real time
- Their icon appears in the top right corner
- Work together simultaneously on any element

## Move project from workspaces

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    In a project, click on "**Settings**"

    ![Menu Project Pn](/images/menu-project.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    In "**Project Settings**", scroll to "**Workspaces**".

    ![Project Change Workspace Pn](/images/project-change-workspace.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    Click "**Change Workspace**".

    ![Change Workspace Pn](/images/change-workspace.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## **Managing access**

- Teammates can access all projects in the workspace
- Switch between different teams from the dashboard side panel
- Teammates need to accept their email invitation to join
